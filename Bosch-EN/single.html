Process improvement with Alteryx at Bosch

During this project, I was working as an intern in the demand planning department at Bosch. As time went by, I identified an opportunity to improve my work and proposed to automate my tasks, as doing them manually was inefficient. After obtaining the corresponding authorization, I was granted access to an Alteryx license and received training on its use through my own means and with the guidance of my supervisor, as well as a designated space in my workday.

Due to our confidentiality agreements, I cannot provide specific details, but I can say that the results of this project were significant. Before automation, I was performing 8 activities, of which I was able to automate 6 in just 2 weeks, significantly reducing my workday from 6 hours to just 3 hours per day.
