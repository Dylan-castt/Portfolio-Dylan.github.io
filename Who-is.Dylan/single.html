Who is Dylan?

With the purpose of expressing myself more clearly, I want to separate my personality into two aspects: one personal and one professional. Despite this, both aspects are interdependent and contribute to the development of myself in the different areas of my life.

Starting with my personal aspect, I describe myself as a sociable and friendly person. I love interacting with other people and learning from them, and I greatly value the time I spend with my family. Additionally, I have a great passion for animals, especially dogs, as I enjoy building relationships based on loyalty, trust, and mutual respect. For this reason, one of my life goals is to establish a foundation to help dogs in need.

Since my childhood, I have always been curious to understand how things work. This curiosity led me to develop a great interest in cars, which in turn motivated me to study engineering. In addition to this, I have always enjoyed exploring and learning new things, which has led me to experiment with a variety of activities. However, my most rooted passion is tennis and ping pong, sports that I love to practice in my free time.

On the other hand, there is my professional facet. Although this dimension of my life has been built from my personal experience, I have strengthened my professional skills mainly through my education and the jobs I have had in different companies. Among the most outstanding competencies that I have acquired are teamwork, leadership, critical thinking, and the ability to communicate effectively. Additionally, following the values instilled in me by my parents, I have learned to be responsible and to always meet my goals. Therefore, I have acquired much of my knowledge about using different software on my own and I always keep myself updated and in constant learning.
